Beginner Level Course

Welcome to the Beginner Level Course on Cryptocurrency. This course is designed to introduce you to the fundamentals of cryptocurrency and blockchain technology. By the end of this course, you'll have a solid understanding of how cryptocurrencies work and how to engage with them safely and effectively.

Lesson 1: Introduction to Cryptocurrency
An overview of what cryptocurrency is, its history, and why it matters.
Lesson 2: Understanding Blockchain Technology
Learn the fundamentals of blockchain and how it powers cryptocurrencies.
Lesson 3: Bitcoin Basics
Dive into Bitcoin, its features, and its role in the crypto ecosystem.
Lesson 4: Altcoins and Tokens
Explore other cryptocurrencies beyond Bitcoin and their unique purposes.
Lesson 5: Setting Up a Crypto Wallet
Learn how to securely store your cryptocurrencies using wallets.
Lesson 6: Buying and Selling Cryptocurrency
Understand the process of purchasing and trading cryptocurrencies safely.
Lesson 7: Cryptocurrency Mining
An introduction to mining and how new coins are generated.
Lesson 8: Security and Best Practices
Tips on keeping your crypto investments secure from threats.
Lesson 9: Legal and Regulatory Considerations
An overview of the legal landscape surrounding cryptocurrencies.
Lesson 10: The Future of Cryptocurrency
Speculations and predictions about where cryptocurrency is headed.
