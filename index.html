Who We Are

The University of Alabama Cryptocurrency Club is an organization that seeks to educate students on the purpose, value, and uses of cryptocurrency and blockchain technology. We are an organization that was founded in 2021, and we have grown ever since. In our meetings, we discuss both current events in the cryptocurrency world, as well as going into detailed concepts regarding cryptocurrency and blockchain technology.
